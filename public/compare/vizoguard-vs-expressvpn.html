Pricing

Vizoguard Basic at $24.99/yr is roughly 75% cheaper than ExpressVPN's 1-year plan at approximately $99.84/yr ($8.32/month). Vizoguard Pro at $99.99/yr is priced almost identically to ExpressVPN, but includes AI threat detection, phishing protection, connection monitoring, and self-healing — features ExpressVPN does not offer at any tier.

Security Features

ExpressVPN is a pure VPN service. It excels at encrypting traffic and hiding your IP address, but it does not analyze threats, detect phishing, or monitor which applications are connecting to the internet.

Vizoguard Pro includes real-time AI threat detection that blocks malicious URLs before your browser loads them — including brand-new phishing domains that appear hours old. Live connection monitoring shows every app talking to the internet. Self-healing protection ensures the security layer cannot be silently disabled. These are endpoint security features that complement VPN encryption.

VPN Performance

ExpressVPN has a clear advantage in VPN performance. With 3,000+ servers in 105 countries, it offers some of the fastest speeds in the industry, excellent streaming support (Netflix, Disney+, BBC iPlayer), and split tunneling to route only specific traffic through the VPN.

Vizoguard currently operates in 1 server region with 100 GB/month bandwidth on the Basic plan. It uses the Outline protocol (Shadowsocks-based), which is designed for censorship resistance rather than raw speed. If fast streaming and global server coverage are your priorities, ExpressVPN is the stronger VPN. If security-first protection with basic VPN coverage is what you need, Vizoguard offers more value.

Privacy

ExpressVPN is based in the British Virgin Islands and has undergone multiple independent audits (KPMG, Cure53) confirming its no-logs policy. It pioneered TrustedServer technology, running all servers on RAM-only infrastructure that cannot store data permanently. ExpressVPN has a strong privacy track record built over a decade.

Vizoguard is operated by PRIME360 HOLDING LTD in Malta (EU) with a strict zero-logging policy. While newer, Vizoguard processes no traffic logs and stores no browsing history. All VPN data is encrypted end-to-end. For users who value a long-established audit history, ExpressVPN has the edge. For users who want security features alongside privacy, Vizoguard fills a gap ExpressVPN does not.
