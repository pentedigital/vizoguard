What Makes Vizoguard Different

Most VPNs encrypt your traffic and stop there. Vizoguard combines VPN with active AI threat detection — catching threats before they reach your device, not just hiding your IP address.

  • ●
    Only VPN with built-in AI threat detection 8 security analysis vectors running in real time — phishing, malware, homoglyph attacks, brand impersonation, and more. No other VPN in this price range does this.
  • ●
    Shadowsocks protocol Uses Shadowsocks (AES-256, ChaCha20) — a censorship-resistant proxy protocol designed to bypass traffic inspection in restricted countries. Works where standard VPN protocols are blocked.
  • ●
    Zero-logging, device-bound keys No traffic logs, no activity records. VPN credentials are bound to the user's device, not floating in a database. Privacy by architecture, not just policy.
  • ●
    Budget-friendly without cutting corners Basic plan starts at $2.08/month (billed annually at $24.99). Full encryption, zero logs, and AI threat scanning at a price that competes with generic VPNs that offer none of the above.
  • ●
    EU-registered, Malta jurisdiction Incorporated under EU law with GDPR obligations — not registered in a jurisdiction chosen to avoid data protection requirements.
